Direct Depositing is easy.
Here’s how:

Download your Direct Deposit Form from your CARD.com card account.

Fill out the amount of your paycheck that you would like to have deposited and then sign your name.

Give the signed form to your human resources department, employer, or your benefits provider to process.

Check with your payer to find out when the direct deposit funds will start. Funds availability is subject to payer's timing.
